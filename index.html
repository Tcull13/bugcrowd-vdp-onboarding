Once you’ve familiarized yourself with our platform, the first step to begin the implementation process is to configure your program settings. Follow the steps below:

  • Log in to your program
  • Assign and update user permissions
  • Update your embedded submission form
  • As soon as you upload the link to your website, your program will be live
